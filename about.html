About XG

This is the About Us page where you can learn more about our website.

XG is a seven-member Hip-hop/R&B-inspired girl group comprising JURIN, CHISA, HINATA, HARVEY, JURIA, MAYA, and COCONA. XG debuted with the single "Tippy Toes" in March 2022 under the global entertainment production company "XGALX," renowned for fostering artist groups with distinctive worldviews and a bold cultural impact, resonating globally.

Their first mini-album, "NEW DNA," released in September 2023, soared to the top of Billboard JAPAN's "Hot Albums" chart. That November, they mesmerized audiences with their inaugural solo concert, "XG 'NEW DNA' SHOWCASE in JAPAN," held at Pia Arena MM.

With a strong presence on the international stage, XG has graced numerous major music festivals across the United States, Australia, Singapore, Malaysia, and the United Arab Emirates.

They've achieved groundbreaking milestones worldwide, including securing the top spot on the U.S. Billboard chart "Hot Trending Songs Powered by Twitter" the first Japanese artists to do so. They also garced the cover of esteemed U.S. magazine "Billboard", again the first Japanese girl group to achieve this.

The name "XG," short for "Xtraordinary Girls," embodies their mission to empower individuals from diverse backgrounds worldwide through their unconventional musical styles and performances, transcending conventional norms and standards.
